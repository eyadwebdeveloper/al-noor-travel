About Al-Noor Travel

Guiding Pilgrims to the Sacred Land Since 2005

Al-Noor Travel was founded with a single, sacred mission: to make every pilgrim's journey to Makkah and Madinah as spiritually fulfilling and physically comfortable as possible. Over nearly two decades, we have had the honor of serving over 25,000 pilgrims from across the world, earning their trust through our meticulous attention to detail, genuine care, and deep knowledge of the holy sites.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Pilgrimage Services

Every aspect of your sacred journey is handled with care, expertise, and devotion.

📋

Visa Processing

Hassle-free Hajj and Umrah visa processing with guaranteed approvals. We handle all documentation, authentication, and submission on your behalf.

✈️

Flight Booking

Premium flight arrangements from over 50 cities worldwide. Choose from economy, business, or first-class seats with top airlines direct to Jeddah or Madinah.

🏨

Hotel Accommodation

Curated 3 to 5-star hotels in Makkah and Madinah, carefully selected for proximity to the holy mosques, quality of service, and cleanliness.

🚌

VIP Transportation

Air-conditioned coaches and private limousines for all rituals including Mina, Arafat, Muzdalifah, and all Ziyarah tours throughout the journey.

🎓

Expert Islamic Guides

Our certified scholars guide you through every ritual with spiritual depth and knowledge. Available in Arabic, English, Urdu, and Bahasa Indonesia.

🍽️

Halal Catering

Delicious, nutritious halal meals prepared by experienced chefs. Catering for all dietary needs throughout your stay in the holy cities.

🏥

Medical Support

On-call medical professionals accompany premium groups. First aid, medication advice, and hospital facilitation available 24/7 during your pilgrimage.

📞

24/7 Support

Our dedicated support team is available around the clock before, during, and after your journey. Reach us by phone, WhatsApp, or our mobile app anytime.
